One-Pan White Cheddar Mac and Cheese

If you can make boxed macaroni and cheese, you can make this one-pan, scratch-made mac and cheese!
The bacon, chives, and crispy bread crumb topping add some great extra flavor and texture.

  • 16 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  1. Combine macaroni, milk, water, garlic salt, and salt in a Dutch oven on a stovetop over medium-high heat.
    Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook, stirring frequently so noodles don't stick to the pan,
    until noodles are soft and most of the liquid has been absorbed, 7 to 10 minutes.
  2. Remove pot from heat and stir in margarine and pepper until melted. Stir in Cheddar cheese and 1/4 cup of the Parmesan cheese until melted.
  3. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
